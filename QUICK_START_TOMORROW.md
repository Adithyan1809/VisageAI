## 💡 PRO TIPS

✓ **Speak slowly** - Give principal time to absorb each slide  
✓ **Make eye contact** - Connect with principal, not the screen  
✓ **Tell stories** - Use real examples (e.g., "When Rajesh enters class, he's detected in 2 seconds")  
✓ **Pause for questions** - After major sections (Slides 5, 12, 18)  
✓ **Smile** - Show enthusiasm for your product  
✓ **Start with pain** - Always begin with problems, then show solution  
✓ **End with action** - Propose specific next step (pilot, meeting, date)  
✓ **Be honest** - If you don't know answer, say "Good question, let me check and get back to you"
