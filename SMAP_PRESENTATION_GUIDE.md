## ✏️ HOW TO CUSTOMIZE

### For PowerPoint (SMAP_CEO_Presentation.pptx):

1. **Contact Information** (Last Slide):
   - Email: Update with your actual email
   - Phone: Add your contact number
   - Website: Add your company website

2. **Team Names** (Slide 14):
   - Find: `CEO/Founder - [Your Name]`
   - Replace with: Actual names and titles
   - Add: Experience details

3. **Company References** (Various):
   - Update company name in intro
   - Add your company logo (use PowerPoint's insert feature)
   - Add customer testimonials with actual names

4. **Financial Figures** (Slide 17):
   - Update revenue projections if different
   - Adjust customer counts
   - Update margins based on your model

5. **Market Data** (Slide 10):
   - Verify TAM/SAM/SOM numbers
   - Update CAGR if different
   - Add links to market research

### For HTML (SMAP_CEO_Presentation.html):

1. Open file in text editor
2. Find and replace:
   - `[Your Name]` → Your name
   - `[Name]` → Team member names
   - `your.email@company.com` → Your email
   - `+1-XXX-XXX-XXXX` → Your phone
   - `www.smap-platform.com` → Your website
3. Save and open in browser

## 🎯 PRESENTATION TIPS

### Before the Meeting:
1. **Test Everything**
   - Open presentation on projector/TV
   - Test audio and video if embedded
   - Check internet connection for web version

2. **Practice Timing**
   - Full presentation: ~15-20 minutes
   - Q&A: ~10 minutes
   - Total: ~30 minutes

3. **Prepare Backup**
   - Bring both PPT and HTML versions
   - Save on USB and cloud
   - Screenshot key slides as backup

### During Presentation:
1. **Engagement**
   - Make eye contact
   - Speak clearly and slowly
   - Pause for questions

2. **Key Talking Points**:
   - **Slide 2**: "Our customers lose $XX million annually to buddy punching"
   - **Slide 3**: "SMAP eliminates this problem with 99% accuracy"
   - **Slide 5**: "Our AI pipeline is battle-tested on 100+ cameras"
   - **Slide 10**: "The market is $10B and growing at 12% CAGR"
   - **Slide 13**: "We're asking for $X million to capture market share"

3. **Demo Time** (if available):
   - Show live dashboard
   - Run face recognition demo
   - Show real-time detection on camera

### Handling Questions:
- **"How is this different from [competitor]?"**
  - Reference Slide 6: Competitive advantages
  - Emphasize cost, speed, and customization

- **"What's your timeline to profitability?"**
  - Reference Slide 17: Year 2 shows $4.5M net income
  - Explain path to profitability

- **"Can this work with existing cameras?"**
  - Reference Slide 4 & 7: ONVIF compatible, existing infrastructure

- **"How accurate is the face recognition?"**
  - Reference Slide 7: 99%+ accuracy in testing
  - Reference Slide 16: Beta results show positive feedback
