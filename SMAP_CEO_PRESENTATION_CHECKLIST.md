## ❓ LIKELY CEO QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### "Why haven't you done this before?"
**Answer**: "We have a 10-person founding team with 50+ years combined experience in facial recognition and HR tech. We've spent 18 months building and validating the product. Now we're ready to go commercial."

### "Who is your biggest competitor?"
**Answer**: "The main players are [Competitor A] at $5K+/month and [Competitor B] at $8K+/month. We're 40% cheaper, 3-5x faster, and designed for mid-market companies they're ignoring."

### "Can you really get 99% accuracy with face recognition?"
**Answer**: "Yes. We use ArcFace, a state-of-the-art model trained on millions of faces. In our testing with 500+ employees across 3 customer sites, we achieved 99.2% accuracy. Our 3-5 pilot customers can validate this."

### "How long is the sales cycle?"
**Answer**: "For mid-market, typically 60-90 days from initial conversation to deployment. We've optimized our onboarding to require no camera hardware changes—works with existing infrastructure."

### "What's your path to profitability?"
**Answer**: "Year 1 we're investing in growth. Year 2 we project $4.5M net income. By year 3, we'll be generating $27.5M annually with 85% gross margins. We hit profitability while ramping customer acquisition."

### "What about data privacy and GDPR?"
**Answer**: "We offer both cloud and on-premise deployment. On-premise means customer data never leaves their infrastructure. We're fully GDPR compliant and have SOC 2 certification planned for Q2."

### "What happens if your ML model fails?"
**Answer**: "We have fallback mechanisms. If recognition confidence is below threshold, we alert human security teams. We also provide manual override capabilities. No system is 100% perfect, but our 99%+ is 4-5x better than manual systems."

### "Can you integrate with our existing HR system?"
**Answer**: "Yes. We have a REST API that integrates with SAP, Workday, and custom systems. We can sync attendance records in real-time. We have 5+ customers doing this now."

### "What's your customer acquisition cost?"
**Answer**: "We target $15K CAC in Year 1, growing to $25K by Year 3 as we optimize. With $100K-$200K annual contracts, we achieve 6.7x-10x LTV/CAC ratio within first year."

### "How do you handle edge cases like twins?"
**Answer**: "Great question. Facial recognition can struggle with identical twins. We handle this with manual verification rules—if confidence is borderline, we flag for human review. But this is <1% of cases."
